About Chuzen Lubes

Chuzen Lubes provides quality engine oils and professional engine servicing with years of trusted experience. Based in Mwanza with branches in Sahara and Mitimirefu, we are your local experts in automotive lubrication and engine care.

We stock a wide range of genuine lubricants for all vehicle types — from passenger cars and SUVs to trucks and heavy machinery. Our trained mechanics deliver every service with precision and care.
